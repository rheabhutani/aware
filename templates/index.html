about the project

In today's world, misinformation is rampant. Many news publication, including reputable and unknown ones, have reliable and unreliable information that often conflicts with each other, leading to a confused and uninformed population.
We at Aware understand the need for reliable facts and data so that you can make decisions based on facts, not fiction.
This is why we are thrilled to present Aware which when given the text of any article will tell you if it's factual or inaccurate. We use state-of-the-art Deep Neural Networks to help classify information.
